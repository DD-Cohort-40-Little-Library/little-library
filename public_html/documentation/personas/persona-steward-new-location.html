User Story

Establishing a new Little Library Location

As a Little Library Steward, Loco just completed construction of his new location and wants to add it to the listing of Little Libraries to promote its use.

User Interaction Flow

Pre-Condition:

Loco is registered as a Steward and has logged into the site and is on the landing page.

Post-Condition

Loco has listed his new Little Library on the site; it appears on the map and can be searched for by other users.

User Interaction Flow

  • Loco scans the landing page and locates the sidebar nav button on the right side of the main element (map) for the "Steward" section and clicks it.
  • Little Library redirects Loco to the "Stewards" page.
  • Loco scans the "Steward" page and finds the Little Library Maintenance menu and selects the "Add a Little Library" button and clicks it.
  • Little Library presents Loco with a form modal with the title "Register a new Little Library" in the center of Loco's screen (need to consider potential pop-up blocking - if that is an issue, will want to redirect to a new page with the form).
  • The form modal is pre-filled with Loco's information as a Steward and provides Loco with required fields including address or latitude-longitude for the location and a photo of the Little Library; optional information includes the option to add a specilization
